Account Creation and Management

This service allows students to create accounts within the system. They provide necessary information such as their name, email address, and a password to create their account. Once created, students can manage their accounts, including updating their personal information and resetting their passwords if necessary. Account management features may also include viewing account activity, such as deposits, withdrawals, and interest accrual.

Deposit and Withdrawal Transactions

Students can deposit money into their savings accounts through various methods such as bank transfers, in-person deposits, or online payments. Withdrawal transactions enable students to access their savings when needed. They can withdraw funds either through an ATM, over the counter at the bank, or via online transfer to another account. These services ensure flexibility and accessibility for students to manage their savings according to their needs.

Interest Calculation and Accrual

The system calculates and accrues interest on students' savings balances periodically. Interest rates may be fixed or variable, depending on the system's settings. Interest calculation may be performed daily, monthly, or annually, depending on the system's configuration.
